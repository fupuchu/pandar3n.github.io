Running Quest

An app idea to get gamers out to work out and stay healthy. Beneficial to them in the long run! Running quest bases on the idea that the user "runs" to finish a quest. Leaderboards and Time-Attack scenarios are currently planned and ultimately, I would want the user to create quests themselves and challenge other "QuestRunners" to finish it! Be it a mile or 10 miles, progress at your pace and become the ultimate runner and in the process become fit!
